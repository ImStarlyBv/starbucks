About the Starbucks Nutrition Calculator

NutriBucks is a free, independent Starbucks calorie calculator covering {{ drink_count }} drinks and food items across hot coffees, cold drinks, Frappuccino® blended beverages, bakery and snacks. Every item lists real nutrition facts — calories, total fat, carbs, sugar, protein, sodium and caffeine — for each cup size from Short to Trenta.

Pick a drink to see its full nutrition table, then customize it: choose your milk, add or remove syrup pumps, drop in an extra espresso shot or top it with cold foam. The calculator adds everything up instantly, so you can hit your calorie and macro goals without giving up your favorite order.
