How the calculator works

NutriBucks starts from the real published nutrition for each Starbucks drink at each size, then adds the estimated impact of your customizations. Swapping milk, adding syrup pumps, dropping in an extra espresso shot or topping with cold foam each adjusts the running totals for calories, fat, carbs, sugar, protein and caffeine.

Customization values are estimates applied on top of the official base nutrition, so treat the total as a close guide rather than an exact label. For the verified base numbers, see each drink's own nutrition page.
